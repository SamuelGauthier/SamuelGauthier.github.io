Logo Design for Andrea Castellón Makeup Artist

2024

A makeup artist, Andrea Castellón, sought to capture the essence of sophistication and simplicity in her brand identity. Collaboratively, we meticulously crafted a minimalist and elegant logo, to reflect their artistry and professionalism.

"Timeless Grace": The logo features a sleek, stylized representation of the name "Andrea Castellón". The minimalist design exudes refinement and sophistication, embodying the artist's commitment to timeless beauty.

Technologies Used: Photoshop, Illustrator
