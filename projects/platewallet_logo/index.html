Logo Design for Platewallet

2019

A Swiss startup in the contactless payment sector needed help in finding their first corporate design. Iteratively worked with the founders from the initial drafts up to the final product, creating a logo and business cards.

"Fast Forward": two chevrons that embody car park entry and exit. Being linked together, they convey the suggestion that velocity is not antithetic to reliability.

Technologies Used: Photoshop, Illustrator
